Scan Student QR Card

Use computer or mobile camera to scan a student's QR card. The system records attendance into the current active session.

How to use

  1. Open Students page and download or show a student's QR card.
  2. Open this QR Scanner page.
  3. Click Start Camera Scanner.
  4. Hold the student's QR card in front of the camera.
  5. The system will automatically open the QR scan result and record attendance.
Important

QR card belongs to the student, not to the class session. The active session is selected automatically by the system.
